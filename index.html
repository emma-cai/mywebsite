About Me

I am a PhD student at Department of Computer & Information Science, Temple University. I started my graduate program in 2011. My advisor is Dr. Alexander Yates. I am interested in Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing and its applications. I am currently working on large-scale textual schema matching and applying the technique to large-scale semantic parsing and question answering.

Projects

Research

My research interest is focused on large-scale information extraction, and developing a question-answering system. Rather than providing a list of webpages in response to a query, our system provides exact answers. For instance, queries like "Which state is Temple University located in?" The system returns exact matching answer. For more information, please click here.
